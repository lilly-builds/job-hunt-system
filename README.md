## ▶️ Try it in 30 seconds (no tools needed)
1. Click the green **`< > Code`** button at the top of this page → **Download ZIP**.
2. Unzip it. Open the folder.
3. **Double-click `index.html`.** It opens in your browser. Done — click around the three pages.

That's the whole thing. Your progress saves automatically in your browser (and each page has a **⬇ backup** button to save a copy to your computer).

---

## ✍️ Make it yours — two ways

**Way 1 — let AI set it up for you (easiest).**
This works with [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) (a free AI coding assistant). If you have it:
1. Open this folder in Claude Code.
2. Type: **"set up my job hunt system."**
3. It asks you a few questions and fills in all three pages + your scorecard. You don't touch any code.

**Way 2 — by hand (no AI, ~15 min).**
1. Open **`your-scorecard.md`** in any text editor and fill in your target role, your must-haves, and your pay floor.
2. In each page, edit the text — every file has a short note at the very top telling you exactly where:
   - **The Map** → change the words in each box.
   - **Daily Scoreboard** → edit the `CONFIG` section (search for `EDIT THIS`) to set your dates and daily tasks.
   - **Aligned Roles** → replace the two example job cards with real ones.

## What the three pages do
| Page | The question it answers |
|------|--------------------------|
| 🗺️ **The Map** | *Where am I actually headed?* — your vision on one page. |
| 🎯 **Aligned Roles** | *Which roles are worth my time?* — a job board scored against **your** criteria, not a generic feed. |
| 📋 **Daily Scoreboard** | *Did I win today?* — a daily checklist for your two big goals, with a streak and a "never miss twice" nudge. |

---

## Your data, your privacy
- **Nothing is sent anywhere.** It all stays in your own browser.
- **⬇ backup / ⬆ restore** on each page saves or reloads your progress as a file.
- Rest days count as a win — the streak keeps growing while you recover.
